This is a voice controlled color picker, click the button down for introductions.

$rgb-colors: [red, green, blue];

After enabling your microphone:

  • “Reset ${rgb-color}”: gets ${rgb-color}.
  • “More/less ${rgb-color}”: adds/subtracts 30% of the ${rgb-color}
  • “A little more/less ${rgb-color}”: adds/subtracts 15% of the ${rgb-color}
